VEENU

Cafe and Restaurant

Veenu is a modern cafe and restaurant concept built around the idea of human connection. The brand's conceptual core is derived from the phonetic breakdown "V-EEN-U" bridging "We" and "You," establishing a welcoming, communal atmosphere.
In a saturated hospitality market, casual dining spaces often struggle with visual fragmentation. They lack a cohesive, foundational narrative that makes the customer feel anchored and truly at home within the space.
We engineered a sun-drenched, organic visual identity utilizing earthy Saffron and Green tones. The logo integrates an inverted, mirrored infinity-loop 'e' to silently communicate continuous trust, warmth, and connection across all physical touchpoints.

02

BAKKENGROW

An Asset Management Company

Bakkengrow is a Crypto Asset Management startup positioned specifically as "Institutional DeFi." The platform is designed to help young Indian professionals seamlessly navigate and build wealth in the digital economy.
The decentralized finance space inherently feels chaotic, highly complex, and untrustworthy to traditional investors who are looking for secure, long-term wealth generation without the stress of daily trading.
We established a sleek, dark-mode brand system. By merging quotation marks (representing clarity) with an origami motif (shaping the future), the visual identity projects absolute institutional precision, trust, and security.

ISRAR STUDIO

Nothing Is Imperfect

Israr Studio is the self-identity system for a strategic creative professional operating at the intersection of brand strategy, narrative building, and high-end visual design.
Design portfolios frequently prioritize aesthetic decoration over underlying logic, ultimately diluting the strategic thinking and problem-solving framework that clients are actually paying for.
We implemented a "Forensic Creative" aesthetic. Driven by clinical layouts, brutalist typography, and a stark palette of Deep Navy and Bone, the system strips away the unnecessary, framing the design work as irrefutable evidence.
